# Sample Lessons & Quizzes

## Learning Path: What is AI?

### Lesson 1: "AI is a Helper"
- **Explanation:** AI is a tool that learns from examples to help people. It does not have feelings.
- **Visual:** Cartoon robot organizing toys by color.
- **Interactive:** Drag toys into "AI sees patterns" bins.
- **Quiz:**
  1. AI learns from: (A) Examples (B) Magic (C) Luck
  2. AI can have feelings: (A) Yes (B) No

### Lesson 2: "Machines Learn Patterns"
- **Explanation:** Machines can find patterns in pictures, sounds, and numbers.
- **Interactive:** Highlight shapes that match the pattern.
- **Quiz:**
  1. A pattern is something that: (A) Repeats (B) Is random

## Learning Path: Image Recognition

### Lesson 1: "Cats vs Dogs"
- **Explanation:** AI can learn to tell cats and dogs apart by looking at examples.
- **Interactive:** Label 6 photos as cat or dog.
- **Mini-game:** Train a classifier and test 3 new images.

## AI Playground Examples
- **Dataset:** Shapes (circle, square, triangle)
- **Training:** Kids label 8 samples.
- **Prediction:** AI guesses the next shape and shows confidence.
